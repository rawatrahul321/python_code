GST Configuration Of Company

Caret IT Solutions Pvt Ltd

Feature

Company creation time or after company creation If GSTConfiguration is True then all Vendor Taxes assign on all product.If GSTConfiguration is False then all related Vendor Taxes of that company is remove into the all product. Product creation time GSTConfiguration of each company is true then all vendor taxes of that company is assign on that product.

*****

Add a GSTConfiguration Boolean Fied.

*****

Company creation time or after company creation set GSTConfiguration is True.

*****

All Vendor Taxes of that company is assign on all product because GSTConfiguration is True on that Company.

*****

Company creation time or after company creation set GSTConfiguration is False.

*****

All Vendor Taxes of that company is remove into all product because GSTConfiguration is False on that Company.

*****

Product creation time GSTConfiguration of each company is true then all vendor taxes of that company is assign on that product.
